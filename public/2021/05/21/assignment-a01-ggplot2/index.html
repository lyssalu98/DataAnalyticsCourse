##Categorical Data Visualization For this assignment, the “quakes” dataset has been selected. Categorical data visualization will be used to present the data, categorized by the numbers of stations that reported the earthquakes.

###Magnitude vs Stations The relationship between the magnitude of the earthquakes and the number of stations that reported them can be seen in this plot. Most often, the larger the magnitude, the more stations reported the earthquake.
